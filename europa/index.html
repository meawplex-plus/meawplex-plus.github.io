Story

Set as a side story of Star Wars and inspired by the movie Europa Report, the Europa campaign is a science fiction campaign that transforms the Dungeons and Dragons world.

In it, you are a band of researchers that are tasked by your company's supercomputer, AXOL, to investigate a moon of Jupiter known as Europa that AXOL has recieved a distress signal from.

The planet is believed to harbor exotic life...

Will your research team find anything on this icy moon?

Gameplay

The gameplay in Europa is different than regular Dungeons and Dragons in several ways:

Instead of standard HP, players have a spacesuit O2 level (percentage) and a suit integrity level (max 30). O2 depletes gradually over time until the player returns to their ship. If a player depletes all of their O2, they die of asphyxiation. If a player's suit integrity drops to zero, they die of the near-absolute-zero temperatures of space flooding their suit.

The action and combat systems are slightly more rigid. To perform a basic action, players must first agree with fellow players (if there is any diasgreement between players, resolve with a highest-roll-take-all D20 roll). Armor and weapons are "locked" by AXOL Command on the ship until players achieve a specified goal or milestone, which will reward them points used to buy newly unlocked things.
